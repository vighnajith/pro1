Master (2021) is an action drama directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, starring Vijay as John Durairaj (J.D.), an alcoholic college lecturer, and Vijay Sethupathi as Bhavani, a ruthless gangster. The plot follows J.D. after he is assigned to a juvenile detention facility for three months, where he discovers that Bhavani is manipulating the young inmates to commit his crimes
